    Who We Are

    The campaign theme for this years World blood donor day is "safe blood saves lives " with the slogan"Give blood and make the world a healthier place."The idea is to focus on the contribution an individual giver can maketo improve health for others in community

    DONATE YOUR BLOOD FOR A REASON, LET THE REASON TO BE LIFE.

    The aim of this camp is to encourage the people to donate blood voluntarily without compensation. the goals of these camp is to raise awareness that individuals can save lives and improve the health of others by donating the blood

    • TIMIMG AS FOLLOWS : 8AM-12 PM Morning session
      2pm-6pm Afternoon session
    • We provide blood donation certificate which is also known as donar card it is a formal document that is provided when an individual has donated blood
    • A blood donation camp is conducted under the leadership of INDIAN RED CROSS SOCIETY(IRCS),blood centre is supported by different Govt.hospitals of Kalaburagi etc.

    We organise blood collection camps in and around karnataka and also we have mobile teams which go out regularly to hold blood donation camps.The mobile teams cover different parts of Bangalore,Mysore,Gadag,Raichur etc.We organize this camps even in schools,colleges and other places with the help of religious,social,political,commercial organizations.
